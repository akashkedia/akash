Research

Events

About Events:

  • Students should be able to join or leave before a cutoff date.
  • At the end of the cutoff date a ranking of the participants should be saved.
  • The top students should then be selected.

Internships

About Internships:

  • Students can state whether they are interested in an internship opportunity or not.
  • Upon stating they are interest to undertake an internship, students can select up to 5 companies they are interested to intern at.
  • Students can state their period of availability for an internship.
  • Students can choose to receive email notifications about an internship opportunity available by any of the top 5 companies selected.
  • Students can choose to be recommended internships based on their coding skills and capabilities.

Github

About Github:

  • Users can input their github account usernames.
  • Track and display users' commits for tracked github repositories.
  • Track and display users' lines of code added and deleted for tracked github repositories.

Courses

About Courses:

  • Allow users to sign up for courses
  • Enabled with a link id for users to share about courses with other users.

Recommendation

About Recommendation:

  • Recommend users badges based on data from other users at school.
  • Recommend users badges based on data from other users with same internship selections.
  • Recommend users badges based on data from other users of same age.
  • Recommend users badges based on data from other users that participated in the same events.
  • Recommend users badges based on data from other users with Github contributions.
  • Recommend users badges based on data from other users with similar badges earned.

Newsfeed

About Newsfeed:

  • Users can see general trending activities on the platform, from their school and of their friends.

Activity Log

About Activity Log:

  • Users can see their own activities such recent badges and tokens earned from third-party sites,github and 1Platform,
  • And events they have joined or withdrawn from.

Lucky Draw

About Lucky Draw:

  • Shows lucky draws with giftaway prizes in a limited time period.
  • Select random winners to receive the giftaway prizes of the lucky draw.
  • Selected lucky draws will have requirements that interested users must fulfil before they can enter the lucky draw.

Wishlist

About Wishlist:

  • Users can view and state prizes they wish to see on lucky draws.
  • Users can boost items on wishlist.

Statistics

About Statistics:

  • Show general statistics of the last 20 badges earned on each of the third-party websites and weekly badges earned by each gender and progress of badges earned by each school.
  • Show internship preference statistics segmented into overall and by company.
  • Show github statistics segmented into overall and by repositories.
  • Show skills breakdown statistics for badges earned by all users.
